Fusion Dance Competition  - Contest Details


Who can apply?

This competition is intended for all semi-professional and professional dancers, as well as creative newcomers. Dance experience

strongly recommended.

 

Date/Location

11/2/2013, 18:00, Arcub Hall, Bucharest Romania

 

Categories

Soloists

Duets/ Troupe

 

Disciplines

ATS / ITS/Gypsy

Medieval style

Tribal Fusion

Styles as Burlesque, Gothic Tribal, Industrial Tribal etc.

Salsa/Flamenco/Tango Fusion

Persian/Indian/Balinese Fusion

Basically anything goes!

The organizers may refuse an application, if the above-mentioend criteria is not respected.

 

Music Lenght

Soloists max. 4 min.

Duets/Troupes max. 5-6 min.

 

Application Process

Use the sign-up link and follow the steps. Don't forget to include a short dance resume & a YouTube link of yourself performing (not be older than 1-2 years),  a description of your style, music, choreography or improvisation. If you don't have a YouTube link please contact us!

 

Application deadline: Oct 1st 2013

You can contact us about applying after the deadline due to possible cancellations.


 

Please note: To enter the competition, you need to sign up at least for 1 workshop!

 

Prizes : To be announced at a later time

Attention: There is no right of appeal.

 

Jury

The contest jury will consist of nationally and internationally acclaimed dancers

 

Assessment criteria

The jury will be assessing aspects like technique & choreographic harmony, versatility of improvisation, the general level

of dancing and range of movements, costume, hair and make-up, musicality, stage presence/ expression, use of stage,

for troupes: group harmony.
