~Bazaar~

 

If you have cool dance related merchandise (costumes, clothing, jewlery, accesories, stickers, DVD's, instruments) and would like to vend at TribalFestBucharest, please consult the guidelines below:

 

The Bazaar spans accross two days:

Nov 2, 2013 - from 12:00 PM till 20:30 PM

Nov 3, 2013 - from 12:00 PM till 22:00 PM

 

All the TribalFestBucharest events take place at ArCub Hall.

 

 

As a vendor you will have access to a beautiful vending venue and a large pool of people (workshop participants, dancers, non dancers).

 

Please note that the rates below apply per vending day:

~ You would need to lease at least 4 sqm for your merchandise at a rate of 10 EUR/sqm.

~ You would need to organize your own vending tables or your own clothing racks

 

 

If you have any questions or special requests, please contact us.
