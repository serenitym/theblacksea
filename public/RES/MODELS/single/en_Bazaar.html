~Tribal Bazaar~

 

 

If you have cool dance related or just creative merchandise (costumes, clothing, jewlery, accesories, stickers, DVD's, instruments) and would like to vend at TribalFestBucharest, please consult the guidelines below:

 

The Festival spans accross two days:

Nov 2, 2013 - from 12:00 PM till 20:30 PM

Nov 3, 2013 - from 12:00 PM till 22:00 PM

 

All the TribalFestBucharest events take place at ArCub Hall. As a vendor you will have access to a beautiful vending venue and a large pool of people (workshop participants, dancers, non dancers).

Please note that the rates below apply to both vending days.

You would need to lease at least 4 sqm for your merchendise (we charge 10 EUR/sqm).

You would need to organize your own vending tables and your clothing racks
